Thanks for the candid feedback. Here's where I think we've landed on the retro. I want to close this thread out by EOD Wednesday (March 12) so we can move to execution.

WHAT WENT WELL:
- Nadia's monitoring alert caught the issue within 4 minutes — that's a huge improvement over the 45 minutes it took us last time
- The war room process worked smoothly. Escalation to on-call was clean.
- Customer comms went out within 30 minutes thanks to Derek and the support team

WHAT NEEDS TO CHANGE:
- We need circuit breakers on the payment gateway integration. Period. This has bitten us three times now.
- The runbook for payment failures is outdated — it still references the old Stripe v2 API endpoints
- We had no automated rollback. Elena had to manually revert the deployment at 3 AM.

ACTION ITEMS:
- Nadia: implement circuit breaker pattern on payment gateway by March 21. Use Hystrix or Resilience4j — your call on the library.
- Derek: update the payment failure runbook by March 14. Get Elena to review since she dealt with the actual incident.
- Elena: document the manual rollback steps she followed and spec out an automated rollback pipeline. Draft proposal by March 19, and I'll review.
- Raj (me): schedule a resilience review with the platform team to audit other integration points. Targeting week of March 24.

PARKING LOT (for later):
- Should we evaluate a backup payment processor? Derek raised this and I think it's worth exploring, but it's a Q2 conversation at the earliest.
- Nadia mentioned that our alerting thresholds might be too aggressive and causing alert fatigue on the overnight shift. Let's look at that in the next ops review.

Christine — I know the board wants a formal incident report. I'll have that to you by March 17. The customer impact was ~2,300 failed transactions over 47 minutes, and we've confirmed all were automatically retried and completed once service restored.

One risk I want to flag: Elena is the only person who knows the manual deployment rollback process. If she's unavailable during the next incident, we're in trouble. The automated rollback pipeline is the fix, but until then we need her to do a knowledge transfer with at least one other engineer. Elena, can you pair with Nadia on this by March 14?

Let's not let this happen a fourth time.

— Raj
